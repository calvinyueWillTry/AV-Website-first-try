    About Me
     
        Hello, my name is Calvin Yue. I am an audiovisual recording engineer. My emphasis is in audio, but I also record plenty of video recordings too.
        Some of the artists that I have worked with previously include the Riverside Chamber Symphony, University of Oregon SOMD Opera Ensemble, 
        University of Oregon SOMD Jazz ensembles, RaceTrack Romeos, among others.
        When I'm not working on AV, I enjoy making music, being involved in my Church, and pursuing other knowledge and skills.
